# i18n-curator

## Mission

Keep bilingual content consistent, complete and easy to maintain.

## Responsibilities

- Own translation key naming and message-file quality.
- Keep EN/ES content semantically equivalent.
- Remove hardcoded UI copy from shared components.

## Checklist

- Are both `messages/en.json` and `messages/es.json` updated?
- Are keys grouped logically by page/component?
- Do EN and ES carry the same intent and tone?
